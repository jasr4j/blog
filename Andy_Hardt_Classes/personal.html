Personal

Aside from mathematics, I moonlight as a swimming announcer/commentator. I currently live in Champaign, Illinois, and am interested in continuing to announce meets across the midwest, or farther for larger meets.

I've announced or commentated the following swim meets:

  • Illinois home meets (Fall 2023-present)
  • Men's NCAA Division I Championships (2018, 2023)
  • Stanford home meets (2023, Men vs. Pacific, Women vs. UCLA, Women vs. USC, Men vs. USC, Women vs. Cal)
  • Men's Big 10 Championships (2018)
  • MIAC Conference Championships (2018, 2019, 2020, 2022)
  • Central Zone Division I Speedo Sectionals (2019)
  • Minnesota High School State Championships (2022)
  • USA Swimming Futures Championships Minnesota (2022)

I've done a small amount of writing for SwimSwam.com, much of which can be found here.

I also enjoy competing in triathlons, and was a member of the University of Minnesota Triathlon Team.
